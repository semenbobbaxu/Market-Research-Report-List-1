<p><p>The sandblasting machine market includes two main cabinet types: dry and wet. Dry sandblasting machines utilize abrasive materials in a dry state, effectively removing rust or paint from surfaces without additional moisture, making them ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, wet sandblasting machines mix water with abrasive materials, reducing dust and achieving a smoother finish, which is beneficial for delicate surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing, addressing various surface preparation needs.</p></p>
