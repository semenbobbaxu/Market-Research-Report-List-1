<p><ul><li>HSP</li><li>HFP</li><li>A2DP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single monaural Bluetooth headset market encompasses three primary profiles: HSP (Headset Profile), HFP (Hands-Free Profile), and A2DP (Advanced Audio Distribution Profile). HSP facilitates basic audio communication, ideal for calls, while HFP enhances functionality by supporting voice commands and better call management. A2DP, on the other hand, allows for high-quality audio streaming, making it suitable for media playback. Together, these profiles cater to different user needs, balancing voice clarity and audio experience in a compact headset format.</p></p>
