<p><strong>The Thermoforming Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoforming plastic market is primarily categorized into three types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed. Vacuum forming involves heating a plastic sheet and using suction to create a shape over a mold. Pressure forming enhances this process by applying air pressure to achieve more intricate and detailed designs. Mechanical forming utilizes mechanical forces, such as stretching and pressing, to mold the plastic, allowing for varying thickness and structural integrity. Each method serves different applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
