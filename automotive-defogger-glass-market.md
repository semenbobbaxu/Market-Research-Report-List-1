<p><p>The automotive defogger glass market comprises three primary types: windscreen, backlite, and sidelite. Windscreens are the front glasses that play a crucial role in visibility and safety by preventing fog and condensation. Backlites refer to the rear windows, often featuring defogging elements to enhance rear visibility. Sidelites are side windows, which may also incorporate defogging technology for improved passenger comfort. Together, these components contribute to overall vehicle safety and driving experience, making defogging essential in automotive design.</p></p>
