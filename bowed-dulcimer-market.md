<p><p>The bowed dulcimer market can be categorized into two primary types: all solid wood and laminated wood. All solid wood dulcimers are crafted from a single type of high-quality wood, offering superior sound resonance and durability. They appeal to musicians seeking rich, authentic tones. In contrast, laminated wood dulcimers are made from multiple layers, providing a more affordable option while still delivering good sound quality. These are ideal for beginners or those on a budget, allowing broader access to this unique instrument.</p></p>
