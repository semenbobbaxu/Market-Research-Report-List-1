<p><strong>The Polypropylene Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li><li>Metalized Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene film capacitors market comprises two primary types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metalized polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors utilize layers of polypropylene as the dielectric, known for their stability and low loss characteristics. In contrast, metalized polypropylene film capacitors incorporate a thin layer of metal on the dielectric, allowing for self-healing capabilities, improved capacitance per volume, and better performance in high-frequency applications. Both types serve various industries, including electronics, automotive, and renewable energy.</p></p>
