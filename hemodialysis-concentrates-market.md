The market primarily includes acid concentrates and bicarbonate concentrates. Acid concentrates contain electrolytes and acids that help balance blood chemistry during dialysis, while bicarbonate concentrates serve to neutralize acids in the blood, providing an essential buffer. Both types are integral to the dialysate used in hemodialysis, ensuring effective patient treatment and optimal dialysis outcomes.</p></p>
