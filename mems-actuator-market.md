<p><p>The MEMS actuator market encompasses several types of technologies. Electrostatic actuators use electric fields to produce motion, ideal for low-power applications. Thermal actuators rely on temperature changes to create movement, suitable for sensors and switches. Piezoelectric actuators utilize material deformation under an electric field for precise control, often used in positioning systems. Magnetic actuators employ magnetic fields for actuation, advantageous in various applications. Other types include acoustic and optical actuators, which serve specialized functions in advanced technologies.</p></p>
