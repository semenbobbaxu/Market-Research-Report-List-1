<p><strong>The In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow IML Containers</li><li>Injection IML Containers</li><li>Thermoforming IML Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Mold Labeling (IML) containers market comprises three main types: Blow IML containers, Injection IML containers, and Thermoforming IML containers. Blow IML containers are created by blowing plastic into a mold where a label is placed, providing a seamless finish. Injection IML containers utilize injection molding to embed labels during the production process, ensuring durability and aesthetics. Thermoforming IML containers involve heating plastic sheets, forming them into molds with pre-printed labels, offering versatility and enhanced visual appeal.</p></p>
