<p><p>External cylindrical grinding machines are categorized into three main types based on their control systems. PLC-controlled machines utilize programmable logic controllers for automation, enhancing precision and efficiency in complex grinding tasks. CNC-controlled machines offer advanced automation through computer numerical control, allowing for intricate part designs and higher productivity. Manually-controlled machines require operator intervention for adjustments, providing flexibility for custom jobs but often resulting in slower processing speeds. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on automation levels and operational complexity.</p></p>
