<p><p>The handheld retinal scanners market is segmented into two primary types: no mydriasis and mydriasis. The no mydriasis segment refers to devices that enable retinal imaging without the need for pupil dilation, allowing for quick examinations with minimal discomfort. In contrast, the mydriasis segment involves devices that require pupils to be dilated for enhanced visualization of the retina, providing more detailed imaging for comprehensive assessments. Both types cater to diverse clinical needs in ophthalmology and optometry.</p></p>
