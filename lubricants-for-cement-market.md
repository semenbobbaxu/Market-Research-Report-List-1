<p><p>The lubricants for the cement market comprise various types designed to enhance performance and longevity. PAO (Polyalpha Olefin) based lubricants offer excellent thermal stability and low-temperature fluidity, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. PAG (Polyalkylene Glycol) based lubricants are water-soluble, providing superior lubrication in wet conditions. Synthetic Hydrocarbon (SHC) based lubricants deliver high-performance characteristics and resistance to oxidation. Additional types include esters and mineral oils, catering to specific requirements within the cement production process.</p></p>
