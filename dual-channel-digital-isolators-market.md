<p><ul><li>Capacitive Coupling</li><li>Magnetic Coupling</li><li>Giant Magnetoresistive GMR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dual Channel Digital Isolators market encompasses three key types: Capacitive Coupling, Magnetic Coupling, and Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) technology. Capacitive Coupling utilizes capacitors for signal isolation, offering high-speed data transmission and low power consumption. Magnetic Coupling relies on transformers to provide robust isolation and high noise immunity. GMR technology, leveraging magnetoresistive materials, delivers exceptional sensitivity and compact designs. Each type addresses distinct application needs in industries like industrial automation, medical devices, and automotive systems, enhancing safety and performance.</p></p>
