<p><p>The neurointerventional device market includes various types designed to treat cerebrovascular conditions. Embolic coils are used to occlude aneurysms. Neurovascular stents, including carotid and intracranial stents, support narrowed vessels. Neurovascular thrombectomy devices remove blood clots from cerebral arteries. Intrasaccular devices work within aneurysms to promote healing. Embolic protection devices safeguard blood vessels during interventions. Other devices may include additional adjuncts or tools used in neurointervention procedures, collectively enhancing treatment options for patients with neurovascular disorders.</p></p>
