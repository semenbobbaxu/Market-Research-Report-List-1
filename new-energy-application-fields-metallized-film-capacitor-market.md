<p><p>The metallized film capacitor market is divided into two key types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are primarily used in alternating current applications, such as power electronics and motor drives, where they help manage voltage fluctuations and improve efficiency. Conversely, DC film capacitors cater to direct current applications, commonly found in energy storage systems, photovoltaic inverters, and electric vehicles, providing stability and energy storage features. Both types play crucial roles in advancing new energy technologies.</p></p>
