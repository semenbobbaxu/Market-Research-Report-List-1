<p><p>The Craniomaxillofacial (CMF) Distraction System market includes various types, notably multi-vector distraction and bi-directional distraction. Multi-vector distraction allows for adjustments in multiple planes to achieve desired skeletal repositioning, making it ideal for complex facial deformities. Bi-directional distraction, on the other hand, facilitates movement in two opposite directions, enhancing bone lengthening and reorientation. Both techniques are crucial in CMF surgery, enabling precise manipulation and promoting effective healing in craniofacial reconstruction and trauma cases.</p></p>
