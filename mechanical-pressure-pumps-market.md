<p><strong>The Mechanical Pressure Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger Pumps</li><li>Piston Pumps</li><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical pressure pumps are categorized into three main types: plunger pumps, piston pumps, and centrifugal pumps. Plunger pumps use a reciprocating plunger to create high-pressure flow, making them ideal for applications requiring precise fluid delivery. Piston pumps, similar in operation, utilize a moving piston within a cylinder to generate high pressure, suitable for various industrial uses. Centrifugal pumps operate by converting rotational energy into fluid flow using a rotating impeller, widely used for transferring liquids in various systems due to their efficiency and simplicity.</p></p>
