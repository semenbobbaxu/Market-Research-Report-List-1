<p><p>Rack and pinion construction elevators are specialized vertical transport systems used primarily in the construction industry. They consist of a platform that is lifted and lowered by a motorized system utilizing a rack (a linear gear) and a pinion (a rotating gear). This design allows for efficient vertical transportation of materials and personnel at construction sites, improving safety and productivity.</p><p>The Rack and Pinion Construction Elevator Market is witnessing significant growth driven by the booming construction sector and urbanization trends globally.
