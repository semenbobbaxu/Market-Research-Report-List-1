<p><strong>The Thick Layer Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Resists</li><li>Negative Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thick Layer Resists market comprises Positive and Negative resists, each serving distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Positive resists undergo chemical changes upon exposure to light, becoming more soluble, which allows for precise patterning. In contrast, Negative resists harden when exposed, making the unexposed areas soluble. The choice between them depends on the required resolution, thickness, and specific fabrication process. As technologies advance, both markets are expanding, driven by increasing demand in electronics, photonics, and microfabrication sectors.</p></p>
