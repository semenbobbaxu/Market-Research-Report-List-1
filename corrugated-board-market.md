<p><strong>The Corrugated Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Face Board</li><li>Single Wall Board</li><li>Double Wall Board</li><li>Triple Wall Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated board market consists of several types based on their structural configurations. Single face board features a single layer of fluting bonded between two liners, offering basic protection for lightweight items. Single wall board has one flute layer and two liners, providing moderate strength and versatility for various applications. Double wall board consists of two flute layers sandwiched between three liners, offering enhanced durability for heavier products. Triple wall board includes three flute layers and four liners, delivering maximum strength for the heaviest shipments.</p></p>
