<p><ul><li>Titanium Nitride Coated Roughing End Mills</li><li>Titanium Carbonitride Coated Roughing End Mills</li><li>Aluminum Titanium Nitride Coated Roughing End Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roughing end mills are categorized based on coatings that enhance their performance and durability. Titanium nitride (TiN) coated roughing end mills offer wear resistance and improved tool life for general applications. Titanium carbonitride (TiCN) coated roughing end mills provide superior hardness, making them suitable for high-speed cutting of tough materials. Aluminum titanium nitride (AlTiN) coated roughing end mills excel in high-temperature environments, offering excellent thermal stability and oxidation resistance, ideal for machining aerospace and automotive components.</p></p>
