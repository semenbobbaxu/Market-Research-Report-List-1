<p><p>The metal sandwich panel market comprises several types based on core materials. EPS (expanded polystyrene) sandwich panels are lightweight and insulated, suitable for cost-effective applications. PU (polyurethane) sandwich panels offer superior insulation and are ideal for energy-efficient structures. Glass wool sandwich panels provide excellent thermal resistance and fire safety, commonly used in industrial buildings. PF (phenolic foam) sandwich panels offer high thermal performance and low smoke emissions, ideal for safety-critical applications. Other market segments include composite and specialty panels tailored for specific needs.</p></p>
