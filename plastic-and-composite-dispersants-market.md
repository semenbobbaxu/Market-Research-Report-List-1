<p><p>The plastic and composite dispersants market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic dispersants. Organic dispersants are primarily carbon-based compounds that enhance the stability and uniformity of polymer composites, often providing better compatibility with organic materials. In contrast, inorganic dispersants consist of mineral-based substances that improve dispersion stability and mechanical properties in various applications. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing performance and enhancing the durability of plastics and composite materials across diverse industries.</p></p>
