<p><p>Vehicle Night Vision Systems (NVS) enhance visibility during low-light conditions. The market can be segmented into two main types: Near-Infrared Ray (NIR) and Far Infrared Ray (FIR) systems. NIR systems use near-infrared light to illuminate the road and enhance visibility for drivers, typically relying on camera technology. In contrast, FIR systems detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, providing a clearer view of pedestrians and obstacles regardless of ambient light. Both technologies aim to improve safety and driving experience at night.</p></p>
