<p><p>The advanced phase change materials (PCMs) market is categorized into three types: organic, inorganic, and bio-based. Organic PCMs typically offer high latent heat storage and are non-toxic, but can be costly. Inorganic PCMs, like salt hydrates, provide higher thermal conductivity and energy storage density but may face stability issues. Bio-based PCMs are derived from renewable resources, promoting sustainability while maintaining effective thermal management properties. Each type caters to specific applications in energy efficiency, building materials, and temperature control solutions.</p></p>
